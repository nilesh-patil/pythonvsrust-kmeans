## Caveats

- Runtime is end-to-end CLI subprocess wall time, including process launch, CSV read, the full k sweep, and output CSV writing.
- Memory is sampled process RSS polled during each subprocess; it is not a platform max-RSS measurement.
- The suite uses single-start k-means++ across all implementations, including scikit-learn `n_init=1`.
- ARI/NMI compare against generated ground-truth labels. Internal metrics are sampled at 10k rows for the largest workloads.
- The Rust and Rust-Parallel paths share the same clustering math; their quality rows should match for paired workloads.
